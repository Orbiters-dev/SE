## 4. Multiple Selection Guide

### When to Apply Which Multiple

| ORBI Scenario | Primary Multiple | Range | Justification |
|---------------|-----------------|-------|---------------|
| Revenue <$5M, profitable | SDE multiple | 3-5x | Small business standard |
| Revenue $5-20M, growing | EV/Revenue | 1.5-3.0x | Growth premium, scale potential |
| Revenue $20M+, profitable | EV/EBITDA | 8-15x | Mature, cash flow based |
| Amazon-dominant revenue | SDE multiple | 2-4x | Amazon aggregator pricing |
| Multi-brand portfolio | Sum-of-parts + premium | +10-20% | Diversification, shared infrastructure |
| Strategic buyer (synergies) | EV/Revenue | 2-4x | Synergy-adjusted premium |
| Financial buyer (PE) | EV/EBITDA or LBO IRR | Based on 20% IRR | Returns-driven |

### Adjustments to Apply

| Factor | Adjustment | Direction |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Growth >20% YoY | +0.5-1.0x revenue | Premium |
| Growth <10% YoY | -0.5x revenue | Discount |
| Negative EBITDA | Use revenue multiples only | N/A |
| Single brand concentration | -10-20% | Discount |
| 10-brand portfolio | +10-20% | Premium |
| Size (<$10M rev vs public comps) | -20-40% | Discount |
| Korea supply chain risk | -5-10% | Discount |
| US market presence + Amazon | +5-10% | Premium |
| Exclusive brand rights | +10-15% | Premium |

## 5. Competitive Positioning Map

### ORBI vs Key Competitors (US Baby Cups Market)

| Competitor | Price Point | Distribution | Origin | Threat Level |
|-----------|------------|-------------|--------|-------------|
| Munchkin | $8-15 | Mass retail + Amazon | US | High (price) |
| NUK | $8-12 | Mass retail + Amazon | Germany | Medium |
| Dr. Brown's | $10-18 | Mass retail + Amazon | US | Medium |
| Tommee Tippee | $10-15 | Mass retail + Amazon | UK | Medium |
| Zoli | $15-25 | Specialty + DTC | US | Medium (overlap) |
| Boon | $12-20 | Specialty + Amazon | US | Low-Medium |
| **Grosmimi** | **$22-37** | **DTC + Amazon** | **Korea** | **Us** |
| Comotomo | $12-15 | DTC + Amazon | US/Korea | Low (bottles, not cups) |
| Elk and Friends | $20-30 | Amazon | US | Medium (glass/SS cups) |

### ORBI Competitive Advantages

1. **PPSU material leadership** — Grosmimi is the premium PPSU straw cup in US market
2. **Korean quality perception** — "K-baby" trend similar to K-beauty
3. **Multi-brand portfolio** — Cross-sell opportunities (cups → snacks → skincare)
4. **DTC + Amazon dual channel** — Reduces platform dependency
5. **High review ratings** — 4.6+ stars on Amazon, strong social proof

### ORBI Competitive Risks

1. **Single product concentration** — Grosmimi cups = ~60% of revenue
2. **Supply chain** — 60-90 day Korea lead times, FX risk
3. **Platform dependency** — Amazon changes (fees, algorithm) impact significantly
4. **Low brand awareness** — vs established Western baby brands
5. **Price premium** — 2-3x competitors, vulnerable to trade-down in recession
